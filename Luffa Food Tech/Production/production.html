Sustainability

Building a greener future through a smart, sustainable food production.

Zero Waste Production

Over 95% of organic waste is diverted to compost or biogas. Damaged items are repurposed into animal feed and residual heat is reused for pre-heating water.

  • Compost & biofuel conversion
  • Feed recovery for livestock
  • Waste-to-energy integration

Renewable Energy Systems

Solar rooftop arrays plus an on-site biogas plant convert food waste into electricity. An AI-powered energy dashboard optimizes usage across the plant.

Solar: rooftop PVs (installed)
Biogas: food waste → electricity

Water Recycling & Harvesting

Smart sensors continuously monitor water purity. 60–70% of process water is treated and reused for cooling and washing; rainwater harvesting reduces municipal demand.

Reuse rate: 60–70%
Rainwater: capture & pump systems

Smart Cooling & Storage

AI-controlled refrigeration with IoT sensors reduces unnecessary cooling cycles and cuts electricity waste by ~40%. Preservation is improved while emissions drop.

AI control for temp
IoT sensors for real-time alerts

Eco Packaging

We use biodegradable materials, seaweed-based edible films and recyclable plant-based plastics. Packaging includes color-change spoilage indicators for consumer safety.

Edible films research pilots
